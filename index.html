About DVText

DVText is a powerful Text-to-Speech (TTS) application that helps you listen to text with a natural, clear, and human-like voice. The application integrates its own browser, supports text input or opens files (.txt, .pdf, .epub) for easy Speech.

Whether you are reading a story, novel, article, study material, PDF or EPUB file, or just want to listen to hand-typed text — DVText is the ideal TTS application to read aloud content smoothly and naturally.

🔥 Key Features

  • ✅ Read text aloud with private built-in browser
  • ✅ Type text or paste content to listen immediately
  • ✅ Open and read aloud .txt, .pdf, .epub files
  • ✅ Support for 50+ languages and 400+ male/female voices, speed and pitch control
  • ✅ Export unlimited audio files

🚀 Why Choose DVText?

  • 🔹 Free & Simple TTS App with clean interface
  • 🔹 Read aloud from browser, typed text, or opened files
  • 🔹 Export audio without limits

  • 🔒 No data collection while browsing
  • ⛔ Block third-party tracking and pop-ups
  • 🔹 Easy-to-use text input tool
  • 🔹 Voice customization: language, gender, speed, pitch

👥 Who is DVText for?

  • Language learners practicing listening and pronunciation
  • Visually impaired users needing text read aloud
